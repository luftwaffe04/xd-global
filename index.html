Azur Lane

Move to Azur Lane Homepage

In a blue planet with 71% covered by the sea, mankind was born, grows, and develops self-civilization in a green blue.

But for them, too, there was a constant ambition and greed. Under the flow of seemingly peaceful world, the undercurrent of history began to flow vigorously.

Finally, in the middle of the unknown equatorial sea, and against the overwhelming power of the enemies, each camp began to fight alone, but the result was very terrible.

Humanity has lost more than 90% of its maritime control, and science and technology and vitality have begun to decline rapidly.

Humanity felt deep frustration with the advent of the sea and unknown enemies, and furthermore, they began to call them Seiren deep in the deep waters.

After a long time, each camp has abandoned its past feelings to regain its glory, and to organize a third military organization, the Azur Lane.

The purpose of the Azur Lane is as follows.

It will collect and share the technical power and resources of each camp, organize elite units capable of fighting against Seiren, and recover the lost seas.
